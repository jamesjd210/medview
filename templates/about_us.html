About Us

We are a group of passionate students that attend the University of Texas at Austin. Amidst the COVID crisis, we saw the opportunity to create a web app that could help people identify where they could get the optimal healthcare. As a young group of ambitious computer scientists, we are striving to create a lasting impression on the world, and MedView is one of the integral stepping stones to our learning and development. Please share this application with close friends and family and thank you for checking out MedView.
